Grzegorz Guśpiel

I am a postdoctoral researcher at Masaryk University in Brno. I work as part of the Laboratory of Discrete Methods and Algorithms (DIMEA), which is led by Petr Hliněný and Daniel Kráľ. I am interested in algorithmics and combinatorics.

I defended my PhD in 2020, in the Theoretical Computer Science group at Jagiellonian University, advised by Paweł Idziak and Grzegorz Gutowski. Between my studies and coming to Brno, I worked as a software engineer at Google.
