Grzegorz Guśpiel

I work as a software engineer at Google Kraków.

I defended my PhD in 2020, in the Theoretical Computer Science group at Jagiellonian University, advised by Paweł Idziak and Grzegorz Gutowski. After my studies, I worked as a software engineer at Google Zürich, as a postdoctoral researcher at the Laboratory of Discrete Methods and Algorithms (DIMEA) in Brno, and as a developer of zero-knowledge proofs at Aleph Zero.

During my time at Jagiellonian University, I was involved in teaching algorithmics to high school and university students, organizing algorithmic workshops for high school students, and preparing problems for programming competitions. I have also served as a reviewer for conferences and journals, including SODA, ICALP, GD, and Discrete Mathematics. In competitive programming, I participated in the ACM ICPC World Finals twice, scoring a bronze medal in 2013.
